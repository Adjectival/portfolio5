About Me

After years of dabbling with building simple websites, I took the plunge into a career as a developer by enrolling in an intensive 6 month course in CSS/JS/Design at Epicodus. In their Frontend Dev track I learned to prototype, to design, and then to build and style web applications. While interning at a small Portland startup last year, I used Bootstrap 4 to make their existing application platform more responsive and easier to use.

Recently I worked as a validation engineer on contract at Intel, which gave me a glimpse of the value of automated testing versus manual. I also discovered that I really enjoy creating and improving documentation.

Because I earned a Bachelor's of Science in Psychology with my military GIBill, I try to channel what I learned about perception and neuroscience as an undergrad when wireframing and designing user interfaces.

Last fall my wife and I finally took our honeymoon, in Seoul!
